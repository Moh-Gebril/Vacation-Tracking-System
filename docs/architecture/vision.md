# System Vision

## Overview

This document outlines the strategic vision, mission, and long-term goals for the system.

---

A Vacation Tracking System (VTS) will provide individual employees with the
capability to manage their own vacation time, sick leave, and personal time off,
without having to be an expert in company policy or the local facilityâ€™s leave
policies.

---

## Business Context and Domain

- **Domain Description**: Vacation time management for enterprise organizations
- **Industry Context**: HR/Human Resources Management
- **Business Area**: Employee leave and time-off administration

---

## Problem Statement

**Current Situation:**
- Manual vacation approval processes cause delays
- HR department bottleneck in time-off management
- Managers lack visibility into subordinate vacation schedules
- Employees uncertain about vacation balance and policies

**Problems to Solve:**
- Streamline vacation request and approval workflow
- Reduce HR administrative burden
- Empower employees to self-manage vacation time
- Enforce complex vacation policies automatically

**Impact of Not Solving:**
- Continued inefficiency in HR operations
- Employee frustration with slow approvals
- Potential policy violations and compliance risks
- Manager time wasted on administrative tasks
